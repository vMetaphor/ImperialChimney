About Imperial Chimney & Masonry

Local, hands-on chimney and masonry service focused on safety, honesty, and clean workmanship.

Locally Owned. Service First.

Imperial Chimney & Masonry was built around a simple idea: treat every home like it’s your own and never cut corners on safety. From chimney sweeps and inspections to full masonry repairs, you get the same level of care on every single job.

When you schedule with us, you’re working with a small, local company – not a big franchise. That means clear communication, realistic expectations, and work that’s actually performed by people who care about their reputation in your neighborhood.

One Company for Your Whole System

  • Chimney sweeping & safety inspections
  • Chimney crowns, caps, liners, and leak repairs
  • Brick, block & stone masonry repairs and repointing
  • Exterior cleaning & power washing for masonry

Whether you discovered a problem during a home sale, a home inspection, or just noticed something doesn’t look right, we can inspect it, explain it, and repair it the correct way.

Safety Every sweep and repair is done with draft, clearances, and fire safety in mind.
Craftsmanship Repairs meant to last — not temporary patches that fail after one season.
Honesty Clear estimates, photos of what we find, and recommendations based on what you actually need.

What You Can Expect

  • Respect for your home — drop cloths and clean-up every time
  • Before & after explanations with photos
  • Itemized, transparent estimates
  • No upselling or unnecessary add-ons
